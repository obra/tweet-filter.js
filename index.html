Spyhunter removes #spymaster tweets from your twitterstream

#spymaster got you down? Don't fret. spyhunter is here to remove tweets containing the hashtag #spymaster from your twitter stream. It's a quick hack of a GreaseKit plugin.

I owe a debt to pbtweet, which served as a template for this quick hack of a script.

To install spyhunter, just install this GreaseKit script. Who knows. It might even work with Greasemonkey.
